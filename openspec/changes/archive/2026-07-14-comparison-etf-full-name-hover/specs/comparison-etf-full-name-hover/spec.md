## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Comparison selector pills show full ETF names on hover
The comparison tab selector pills SHALL expose the full ETF name in a hover tip while preserving the existing ticker label and checkbox behavior.

#### Scenario: Hovering a comparison selector shows the full ETF name
- **WHEN** the user hovers over a comparison selector pill or checkbox
- **THEN** the full ETF name SHALL be shown in the hover tip
- **AND** the visible selector label SHALL remain unchanged
